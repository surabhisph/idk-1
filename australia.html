Australia! Australia is a continent as well as a country and it's known for its kangaroos and koalas. It surrounds itself with the Indian and Pacific Ocean and New Zealand, Indonesia, New Calidonia, Papau New Guinea, Vanuatu, Soloman Islands, and Timor-Leste. It's not only known for it's wildlife, but also it's beaches. About 85% of this archipelago live within 50 kilo-meters of the coast.
